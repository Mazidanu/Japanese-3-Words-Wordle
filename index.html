Welcome to the Game!

The Hiragana Wordle game is a word-guessing game where players attempt to guess a fixed three-letter Japanese word represented in Hiragana. Players have a limited number of attempts to guess the correct word, and the game provides feedback on each guess to help them succeed.

Players enter their guesses using either a text input field or a virtual Hiragana keyboard displayed on the screen.

Once the player has entered a guess (a three-letter Hiragana word), click a "Submit" button to check their guess.

Inspired by the popular Wordle created by the original creator of Wordle, Josh Wardle from New York Times!
